OS X Terminal Internationalization

Jun 5th, 2007

<p>I have a problem and I have high hopes that this might solve it. A little background is in order. To manage our internal documents we are using Subversion. A small <em>svn update</em>, and all documents are up to date, a small <em>svn commit</em> with a comment and your new version is in. All cool.</p>
