Your Job Blog

Nov 23rd, 2004

<p>You might wonder if I will now talk about Blogger Jobs but that’s not the case. I am actually just talking about the fact that one reason to keep a blog is that it’s a wonderful engine to drive intrest in yourself and to further your connections. It is very likely that you will get a job offer via your blog or that it will at least help you in getting the right job for you. This is very evident in a series of posts you can find on Jeremy Zawodny’s blog. It started with Russel Yahooing soon, follow by Jeremy Cole doing it too!. Russel talks a bit more deeply about his move to Yahoo!, which is quite interesting. Jeremy now finished it off with his post on title="Blogs as Professional Lubricant (Jeremy Zawodny’s blog)">Blogs as Professional Lubricant<br /> with which I can very much agree.</p>
