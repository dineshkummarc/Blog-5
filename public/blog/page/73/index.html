Nokia Nseries Announced

Apr 27th, 2005

Nokia has just announced the <a title="Nokia | Nseries" href="http://www.nokia.com/nseries/">Nokia Nseries</a>. Christian writes a bit about the N90, which sports a Tessar lens from Zeiss, coming in at 2M pixels. Some really nice ones, including the N91 which has 4GB storage for music. I wondered what Nokia would do in the future as they ran out of numbers for their phones. Now I have it. Nxx :)
