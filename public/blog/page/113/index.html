Skype for MacOS X

Aug 31st, 2004

Now that I am home, I swiftly downloaded Skype for MacOS X to find out a) that it is very nice, b) that is doesn’t take over the contacts I have on the windows box at work, c) that search somehow doesn’t fully work or all my contact are hidden somehow. But it seems to be working and once again I prefer it to the windows version. My nickname is othylmann if you want to be able to start a chat some time in the future. I am looking forward to see how this develops. What is very nice I have to say is that I see tons of bloggers popping up say "Yay! Finally, a MacOS X Version of Skype. Downloading now." This proves once again that MacOS X is extremely popular among bloggers. Great crowd to be with :)

To All Friendster Users

Aug 31st, 2004

I never ever signed up for Friendster. I don’t know why. Especially because now I have LinkedIn and OpenBC accounts (and an Orkut account which I hardly use). But anyway. Friendster fired Joyce Park because of her blogging. As a blogger, Joyce talked about the switch to PHP for Friendster, got Slashdotted and in general got a good amount of attention. But she really didn’t reveal anything secret.

Now the bloggers fight back. Oh the fun :) Jeremy Zawodny started it and is now asking everyone to cancel their Friendster Accounts (see here) with the reason: "Fireing employees for blogging". Now go cancel your account :)
