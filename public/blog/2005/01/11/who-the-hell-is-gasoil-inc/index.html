Jan 11th, 2005

Oh, and Eric Schmidt is CEO and Wayne Rosing is a poor guy as he is Senior VP at Google but only VP at Gasoil Inc.. Larry Page is obviously also President of Gasoil Inc and also CTO of Island Pac Inc.). Gasoil Inc.’s adress can be found here. Both are not mentioned in the Google SEC filing from what I can see.
